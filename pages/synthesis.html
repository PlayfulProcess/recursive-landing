What Are Recursive Patterns?

Recursive patterns emerge when simple rules or elements repeat and combine with themselves, creating increasingly complex and beautiful structures. Like fractals in nature, each level of the pattern contains the seeds of the whole.

They represent a fundamental principle of how complexity emerges from simplicity - how meaning-making itself might work recursively, with each understanding building upon and transforming previous ones.

Recursive Patterns Playground

Fernando Carnauba's Combinatorics series explores how unit squares can be assembled into countless variations. In paintings with 96 pieces, the number of possible ways to assemble a painting surpasses by far the number of atoms in our known universe.

The difficulty in selecting and encoding patterns that are actually harmonious is like the artist's, for me, a kind of experiment to prove that there is an underlying cosmic intelligence (or artist) in nature that rules from the tiniest particle to the largest cosmic bodies.

Philosophical Pillars

I cannot claim to be an expert, only a student. But I do want to learn in public so that I can find real meaning, and not just make belief. But I do recognize that sometimes it is hard to differentiate them and I like to think of the imagination exercise required to make meaning as a recursive make belief process, in which hypothesis get experimented with in public or against our experiences.

The recursiveness itself lies in the public-private dialogue intrinsic to Kantian philosophy - finding what is fair through continuous reflection. Recursive.eco serves as my public space (addressing the public as a non-private guardian), while PlayfulProcess remains more personal and exploratory.

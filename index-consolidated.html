What is Jongu?

An experiment in recursive public technology where every technical decision is a moral decision.

We Transform the Collective by Transforming Ourselves

Jongu is a space where human wisdom meets artificial intelligence, where ancient practices inform digital tools, and where personal transformation becomes collective evolution. Named after the Brazilian Jongo dance‚Äîwhere participants take turns leading in a circle‚Äîwe're building technology that honors both individual expression and community rhythm.

We believe that the question between whether AI is going to replace humans or not is a false dilemma. The best way to predict the future is to invent it. If we want AI to augment, rather than replace humans, we need to build the software that will reflect this principle.

Open, Not Free

Open: Everyone in this community has a say in this community
Not Free: You must follow the rules to be part of this community
Transparent: We are building AI-mediated governance that monitors compliance according to the rules we build together
Earned Trust: The recursive public grows as trust is demonstrated

This business model is designed to sustain infrastructure for the commons, not extract value from users.

ü§ñ Collective AI Funding (Planned)

Instead of individual AI subscriptions, we're designing a collective pool system. Contributors support the commons, not their individual usage. Everyone benefits from AI capabilities regardless of individual contribution. This keeps AI tools accessible while sharing costs collectively.

The Recursive Principle: Every revenue stream directly sustains free infrastructure.

This isn't freemium (where free users funnel to paid). It's recursive: edge activities fund core commons.

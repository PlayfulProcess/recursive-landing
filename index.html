Why I'm Building This

The path that led me here wasn't about finding the perfect business model‚Äî it was about recognizing a fundamental need for accessible tools in our digital age.

1

The Access Realization

While pursuing therapy credentials, I discovered the mental health system's barriers go far beyond pricing. The entire system creates barriers that might protect some but definitely exclude many. This is why I'm starting with wellness channels, but the same holds true for several other segments.

If we're entering an age where AI will be key for people to not go obsolete and we're anchoring price in dollars, that also excludes many from the new age that is dawning on us. Meaningful tools should be accessible based on need, not gatekeeping.

2

The Connection Crisis

Most digital platforms optimize for engagement through endless scrolling and passive consumption. People have thousands of "connections" but feel increasingly isolated.

What if platforms were designed for collaborative creation instead of competitive consumption?

3

The Vision Crystallizes

The solution wasn't just building another platform‚Äîit was creating conditions where authentic community could emerge. When people aren't being monetized or surveilled, when access isn't restricted by economic ability, they can focus on genuine connection and collaborative creation.

Jongu became my attempt to test whether digital spaces could prioritize human flourishing over profit maximization.

Key Questions & Enablers

These aren't just technical features‚Äîthey're fundamental enablers that could make authentic digital community possible. Each principle addresses a barrier to genuine connection.

Donation-Sustained Access

Key Question: Could a platform be sustained entirely through voluntary donations, ensuring people can access resources based on need rather than economic ability?

Why This Matters: When access isn't tied to purchasing power, people can focus on genuine contribution rather than financial transactions. A $10 tool feels very different to someone in Brazil versus Silicon Valley.

Privacy by Design

Principle: People can navigate and use all tools without any data collection. No tracking, no profiles, no surveillance.

Why This Matters: When people aren't being monitored and monetized, they can be authentic. True connection requires psychological safety from surveillance.

‚úÖ Implementation: No cookies, no tracking scripts

Optional AI Enhancement

Principle: AI assistance is always optional. All self-hosted tools provide full value without requiring AI interaction.

Why This Matters: People should have agency over their relationship with AI. Some want assistance, others prefer pure human-to-human interaction.

‚úÖ Current: AI chat is opt-in feature

Calm Platform + Deep Connection Spaces

Approach: Jongu itself remains calm and accessible. For those wanting deeper connection, Discord channels offer collaboration, notifications, and habit-building support.

Why This Matters: Different people need different levels of engagement. Some want quiet reflection, others want active community involvement.

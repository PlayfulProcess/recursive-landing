Recursive Meaning-Making

Making belief recursively to find meaning. Following Joseph Campbell's call to reinvent our mythology, I'm building new sense-making tools through software and content.

There's no alternative to following your bliss—just unhappiness. So I'm following mine: making belief recursively to find meaning, while meaning good for the whole. This is my echo chamber for the make-belief world I'm theorizing and experimenting with in my meaning-making attempt.
